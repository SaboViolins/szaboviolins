Welcome to The Workbench

Welcome — and thank you for stopping by.

This is a new corner of my website where I hope to regularly share what is happening in the workshop: instruments currently in progress, the wood I am selecting and preparing, experiments with varnish, and now and then simply a few thoughts on sound, craft, and the long tradition this work belongs to.

I hope this becomes a small window into the daily life of a luthier — and I look forward to sharing many more posts here in the months and years to come.
